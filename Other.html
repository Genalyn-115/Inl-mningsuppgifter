Skiing

I never imagined skiing in my entire life because in the Philippines it's not snowing at all. However, I learned it 8 years ago and I found it so cool and enjoying. Yes, skiing is quite expensive but you can also find cheap ski resorts which are offering discounts. When it comes to skiing gears you can buy second hand which is environmentally friendly and budget friendly as well.
